#+TITLE: Hiring is a guess. Firing is a choice.
#+date: 2026-01-30
#+DRAFT: false
#+TAGS[]: startups
#+CATEGORIES[]: tech

You can't hire with certainty.

Things move too fast to be absolutely sure of everyone. And if you're certain of everyone it's because you're looking at the same signals you know. You're not allowing for adding something new, something different.

You can fire with certainty.

After people have been in your company for a month or two, you have a pretty good idea what they're about.
